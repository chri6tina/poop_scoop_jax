Poop Scoop Service FAQ - All Your Questions Answered

Everything you need to know about our Jacksonville dog waste removal services

üí∞ Pricing & Cost Questions

How much does poop scooping cost in Jacksonville?

Our Jacksonville poop scoop service starts at $15 per visit for weekly service, $18 for bi-weekly, and $25 for one-time cleanups. Prices vary based on yard size, number of dogs, and additional services.

Do you offer discounts for multiple dogs?

Yes! We offer volume discounts for multiple dogs. 2 dogs get a 10% discount, 3 dogs get 15% off, and 4+ dogs get 20% off your total service cost.

Are there any hidden fees or surprise charges?

Never! We believe in transparent pricing. All costs are discussed upfront, and you'll receive a detailed quote before we start any work. No hidden fees, no surprises.

Do you offer payment plans or subscriptions?

Yes! We offer monthly billing for regular customers, and you can pay weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly. We accept all major credit cards and can set up automatic payments for your convenience.

üìç Service Areas & Coverage

What areas of Jacksonville do you serve?

We serve all of Jacksonville including Ponte Vedra, San Marco, Riverside, Avondale, Mandarin, Arlington, Downtown, and surrounding areas. Contact us to confirm service in your specific neighborhood.

Is there a travel fee for certain areas?

No travel fees within our standard service area! We include travel costs in our base pricing for all Jacksonville locations. Some outlying areas may have a small surcharge, which we'll discuss upfront.

Do you service commercial properties?

Yes! We service dog parks, apartment complexes, veterinary clinics, and other commercial properties. Commercial rates start at $50 per visit with volume discounts available.

üîß Service Details & Process

What exactly do you do during a poop scoop service?

We thoroughly clean your entire yard, removing all dog waste, bag it in eco-friendly bags, and dispose of it properly. We also clean up any missed spots and can provide deodorizing or hose-down services upon request.

How long does a typical service take?

Most residential services take 15-30 minutes depending on yard size and amount of waste. We work efficiently while being thorough, and we'll give you an estimated time when we arrive.

Do I need to be home during the service?

No! We can service your yard whether you're home or not. We'll text you when we arrive and when we're finished, and you can choose to receive before/after photos.

What if it's raining on my scheduled day?

We work in light rain! If weather is severe, we'll contact you to reschedule at no charge. Your safety and our team's safety are our top priorities.

üìÖ Scheduling & Frequency

How often should I have my yard cleaned?

Most customers choose weekly service for optimal yard health. Bi-weekly works well for smaller yards with 1-2 dogs, while twice-weekly is perfect for large yards or multiple dogs.

Can I change my service frequency?

Absolutely! You can change your service frequency anytime with 24 hours notice. We're flexible and want to work with your schedule and needs.

What if I need to skip a week or pause service?

No problem! Just give us 24 hours notice to skip or pause service. We understand life happens and we're here to accommodate your needs.

Do you work on weekends?

Yes! We work Monday through Saturday, 7 AM to 7 PM. Sunday service is available 8 AM to 6 PM for an additional $5 fee.

üêï Special Situations & Scenarios

What if my dog has diarrhea or loose stools?

We handle all types of dog waste, including diarrhea and loose stools. We use special tools and techniques to clean these messes thoroughly and sanitize the area.

Do you clean up after puppies or elderly dogs?

Yes! We're experienced with all ages of dogs. Puppies often have more frequent and smaller messes, while elderly dogs may have accidents. We handle both with care and understanding.

What about multiple dogs with different schedules?

We work with all your dogs' schedules! Whether they go out at different times or have different areas of the yard, we'll clean everything thoroughly during our visit.

Do you handle aggressive dogs or special needs?

We're experienced with all types of dogs. If your dog is aggressive or has special needs, just let us know and we'll work around their schedule or use special techniques to ensure everyone's safety.

What if my yard has difficult terrain or obstacles?

We work around pools, gardens, play equipment, and difficult terrain. We have specialized tools for tight spaces and will work carefully around your yard's unique features.

Do you clean up after other animals too?

We specialize in dog waste removal, but we can also clean up after cats, rabbits, and other small pets. Just let us know what animals you have and we'll adjust our service accordingly.

üè• Health & Safety

Is your service safe for my family and pets?

Absolutely! We use pet-safe, eco-friendly products and follow strict hygiene protocols. All our equipment is sanitized between visits, and we wear protective gear to prevent cross-contamination.

What do you do with the waste after collection?

We dispose of all waste in accordance with local regulations, using biodegradable bags. We never dump waste in storm drains or public areas, and we follow all environmental guidelines.

Do you use any chemicals or harmful products?

No! We use only pet-safe, eco-friendly products. Our deodorizing sprays are non-toxic and safe for pets, children, and plants. We're committed to keeping your family and environment safe.

üöÄ Getting Started

How do I get started with your service?

Simply contact us for a free quote! We'll ask about your yard size, number of dogs, and preferred schedule, then provide a detailed estimate. No obligation, no pressure - just honest pricing.

Do you offer a satisfaction guarantee?

Yes! We guarantee your satisfaction with every service. If you're not completely happy, we'll return within 24 hours to make it right at no additional charge.

Can I try your service before committing to regular visits?

Absolutely! We encourage new customers to try our one-time service first. This lets you see our quality and decide if regular service is right for you.
