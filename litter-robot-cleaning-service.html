How We Clean Your Litter Robot

Our comprehensive cleaning process ensures your automated litter box stays fresh and functional

1

Initial Assessment

We thoroughly inspect your litter robot for any mechanical issues, sensor problems, or areas requiring special attention.

2

Complete Disassembly

Carefully disassemble all removable components including the globe, waste drawer, bonnet, and base unit.

3

Deep Sanitization

Use professional-grade sanitizers and cleaning agents to eliminate bacteria, odors, and residue from all surfaces.

4

Mechanical Maintenance

Clean and lubricate moving parts, check sensors, and ensure all mechanical components are functioning properly.

5

Reassembly & Testing

Carefully reassemble all components and test all functions including cycling, sensors, and connectivity features.

6

Quality Assurance

Perform final testing and provide you with a detailed service report and maintenance recommendations.
